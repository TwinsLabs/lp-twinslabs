Clicca ClinicOS — what Twins Labs builds

Clicca ClinicOS is our cloud platform for small and mid-sized medical clinics in Brazil. We bring clinical and financial operations together in one place, cutting system fragmentation and rework for staff. See the live product at clicca.com.br.

Market and opportunity

Thousands of clinics in Brazil still run on disconnected tools, manual processes, and low standardization. There is room for an integrated platform that fits the SMB segment and matches regulatory reality and how Brazilian patients behave (including heavy use of WhatsApp).

Healthcare SMB segment

Smaller clinics need full software without hospital-grade complexity — and predictable cost.

Fragmentation today

EHR, scheduling, finance, and communication often sit in different systems: hidden cost and operational risk.

Cloud and AI adoption

Structural trend: cloud operations and patient engagement automation are becoming baseline, not a nice-to-have.
